# Thai Nang Tani Politics Observatory

Welcome to the Thai Nang Tani Politics Observatory — an independent, long‑term project dedicated to tracking narrative shifts, media framing, and political communication in Thailand.

This observatory documents:
- How media outlets frame political events  
- How narratives evolve over time  
- How public communication changes in response to policy, crises, and power dynamics  
- How visa policies and migration discourse are shaped in Thai media  

Our goal is to create a transparent, structured archive that helps readers understand not only *what* is being reported, but *how* and *why* it is being framed in a particular way.

---

## 🔍 What You Will Find Here

### **1. Narrative Tracking**
Continuous documentation of how key political topics are framed across different media sources.

### **2. Media Mechanics**
Analysis of editorial choices, omissions, angles, and linguistic framing.

### **3. Visa Policy Timeline**
A chronological record of official statements, media reactions, and public sentiment.

### **4. Archive**
A structured, searchable collection of all observations, sorted by topic and date.
